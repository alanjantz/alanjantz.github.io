About me

My name is Alan Jantz. I am a software developer focused on backend development with C#, but sometimes I like to explore and challenge myself with frontend adventures.

I have always liked to discover new things and trying to understand how they work is one of my oldest hobbies. When I was younger, I started a HTML & CSS blog and played with my imagination to create a lot of designs.

In 2017, I started a C# Programming Course and in that same year I started to study Computer Science on college. Thanks to it, I was able to start my professional life as a software developer.
